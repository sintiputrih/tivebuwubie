Drug Controversy Continues Between Linda &amp; Hulk Hogan

Publish date: 2024-02-02

HULK HOGAN’s estranged wife has won the latest round in the public battle with her wrestler ex – drug tests he demanded she take have come back negative.

The tough guy told Linda Bollea to take the test after she claimed to have caught the star – real name Terry Bollea – "rolling and smoking joints" with their daughter Brooke’s boyfriend.

He denied the allegations and turned on his wife, demanding she prove she was clean. He said, "It needs to be done, and she’ll fail it."

Linda took him up on the offer and underwent a full drug screening and "tested negative for everything", including amphetamines, marijuana, cocaine and opiates such as Vicodin and heroin, according to her representative Gary Smith.

He says, "Linda also had a full medical exam. According to the doctors who examined her, she has even had gains in bone density and muscle mass since her last physical, which indicates a healthy lifestyle.

"This further disproves Hulk’s and Brooke’s unfounded and hurtful allegations. Linda’s drug screening shows she has no trace of any drugs in her system, legal or illegal, that includes prescription medication. Linda does not do drugs and never accused her daughter of doing drugs either. Hulk is the expert on drug use."
 
Linda filed for divorce in 2007 after 24 years of marriage.
