The Real Housewives of Potomac TV Episode Recaps &amp; News

Publish date: 2024-07-08

The wealthy town of Potomac, Maryland, is just outside of Washington DC, and there’s drama to be found there. The Real Housewives of Potomac follows the personal and professional lives of a group of women. Some of the Housewives who have been featured on the show include Ashley Darby, a Miss District of Columbia Pageant winner who became a stepmom to a 21 and 24-year-old when she was just 22; philanthropist Gizelle Bryan; and Robyn Dixon.
