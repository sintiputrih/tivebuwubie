Beyonce's Ruby Red Dress Features a Hip-High Leg Slit

Publish date: 2024-05-20
  • Having attended The Lion King's London premiere wearing a yellow-gold gown featuring a revealing leg slit, Beyoncé has outdone herself by wearing a gorgeous red dress with an even higher cut.
  • Beyoncé posted photos of the glittery red gown on Instagram and posed alongside her husband, Jay-Z.
  • The superstar voices Nala in Disney's The Lion King.

Beyoncé knows exactly how to make a lasting impression, and her recent outfit choices have been nothing short of flawless.

Related Story

Having already shown some serious flesh at the premiere of The Lion King in London, Beyoncé took to Instagram to showcase another revealing look. This time, the gorgeous one-shoulder gown featured sheer draping, intricately embroidered material, and an incredibly high leg slit. The singer paired the custom design by Walter Collection with Jimmy Choo Viola heels and Messika Paris jewelry.

She posed with husband Jay-Z, who wore an eye-catching white tuxedo. The pair looked impeccable as they attended a Great Gatsby-themed party in honor of Beyoncé's niece who was turning 21.

Jimmy Choo Viola Feather Tassel Sandals

Beyoncé also posed solo in the gown, showing how perfectly the dress's bodice fit her figure and giving fans a glimpse at just how high the slit rose.

At the royal premiere of Disney's The Lion King, the superstar rocked a glistening Cong Tri gown, also featuring an impressive leg slit.
