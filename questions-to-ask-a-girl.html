114 Best Questions To Ask A Girl To Know Her Better in 2024

Publish date: 2024-03-10

These questions to ask a girl won’t steer you wrong. Spend some time asking them, and by the end of the conversation, you’ll be reading her like an open book.

Despite contrary belief, most girls really aren’t that complicated. Sure, they can come off like mystical creatures (even though they’re humans—just the opposite sex), but if you want to know something, most of the time, all you have to do is ask.

Coming up with the right question to ask a girl can show her you really care. Whether you’re aiming to understand her on a deeper level or just want to get to know her in the first place, the best questions for girls will reveal plenty and, more likely than not, lead to an epic conversation and a deeper level of understanding.

If you want to grasp who she really is, these are the best things to ask a girl, from a girl herself.

Key Takeaways

The best questions to ask a girl range from standard get to know you questions to more complex personal queries, all with the spirit of understanding her better. Dig deeper with intimate questions to ask a girl, or keep it lighthearted with funny inquiries or flirty quips showing your true personality.

It really all boils down to timing. The questions to ask her can be pulled out at any moment, while more scandalous or deeper queries should be reserved for later down the line.

Keep reading to discover everything you need to know about the best questions to ask a girl so you’re never left without a conversation starter again.

Best Questions to Ask a Girl

After dating for ten-plus years and going on more first dates than you could ever imagine, I’ve learned a thing or two about the best questions to ask a girl.   • What’s at the top of your bucket list?

  • What are you looking forward to?
  • What’s your favorite show to binge-watch?
  • Do you prefer to spend or save money?
  • What are your favorite clothing brands?
  • Where was your favorite vacation?
  • What words of advice do you live by?
  • Do you have any guilty pleasures?
  • What’s the craziest thing you’ve ever done?
  • Where did you grow up?
  • Where’s your favorite place on earth?
  • What’s your greatest accomplishment?
  • What are you most passionate about?
  • Do you like to dress up or keep it casual?
  • What inspires you?
  • What’s something I would never guess about you?
  • Are you a planner or like to be planned for?
  • Would you rather relax on vacation or do activities?
  • What’s one thing you could never give up?
  • What are your pet peeves?
  • Would you rather have a big night out or a cozy night in?
  • What’s the best gift you’ve ever received?
  • Would you ever live abroad?
