Publish date: 2024-07-16

Bio:
Taylor Swift (born Taylor Alison Swift in Reading, Pennsylvania on December 13, 1989) is an American country pop singer-songwriter, musician, and actress. Starting her career in 2006, Taylor released her debut single “Tim McGraw” along with her self-titled debut album, which went on to multi-platinum after a stellar debut. In November 2008, she released her second album, Fearless, and earned four Grammys, including Album of the Year. In 2009, Taylor was the involved with Kanye West‘s famous MTV VMA stunt, interrupting Taylor’s win for Best Female Video to declare Beyonce‘s “Single Ladies” to be superior to Taylor’s winning video for “You Belong With Me.” She followed her success with her third album Speak Now on October 25, 2010, selling over a million copies in the first week. In 2012, Taylor released her fourth studio album Red, which sold 1.21 million units in its first week! She has been listed in the 2012 Guinness Book Of World Records as the Fastest Selling Digital Album by a Female Artist for her album Speak Now, and Most Simultaneous U.S. Hot 100 Hits by a Female Artist. She also appeared in films as an actress, starring in the 2010 romantic comedy Valentine’s Day and in the animated feature The Lorax in 2012. In 2014, Swift’s sound made a complete transition to pop, and she released the pop/synthpop album 1989 in October. It sold over a million copies in its first week of release, and Swift began a worldwide tour for the album in 2015. Her sixth studio album, Reputation, was released on November 10, and it also sold over 1 million copies in its first week. Her Reputation Tour kicked off on May 8, 2018.

Best Known For:
Taylor is best known as a singer-songwriter, releasing such hits as “We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together,” “Blank Space,” and “Shake It Off.”

Personal Life:
Taylor is currently dating British actor Joe Alwyn. Her string of A-list dudes includes Tom Hiddleston, Calvin Harris, John Mayer, Jake Gyllenhaal, Joe Jonas and Taylor Lautner, many of whom she’s written songs about post-relationship. Taylor’s relationship with One Direction member Harry Styles is the subject of Swift’s single “I Knew You Were Trouble.”
