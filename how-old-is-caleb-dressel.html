How old is Caleb Dressel?

Publish date: 2024-01-28

Caeleb Dressel was born on August 16, 1996, in Green Cove Springs, Florida. As of February 2024, he is 27 years old.

Early Achievements

Caeleb Dressel's talent in swimming surfaced at a young age. By the time he was a teenager, Dressel had already begun breaking records. His exceptional performances in various swimming events, such as the 50-meter freestyle and 100-meter free, showcased his extraordinary abilities.

Record-breaking Performances

Throughout his career, Dressel has achieved numerous milestones. Notably, in the 2013 Winter Junior Nationals, at just 17 years old, he achieved a sub-19 second swim in the 50-yard free, setting a new 17-18 National Age Group Record. His exceptional speed and versatility in different swimming disciplines have consistently set him apart in the world of competitive swimming.

Olympic and World Championship Titles

Dressel's remarkable career includes 7 Olympic titles, 13 World Championship titles (LCM), and 6 World Championship titles (SCM). His outstanding performances have earned him a total of 11 US National Championship titles and 10 US NCAA titles. Furthermore, at the 2017 World Championships, Dressel secured seven gold medals, an achievement that tied him with Michael Phelps for the most gold medals won at a World Championship meet. His versatility in swimming disciplines, including freestyle, breaststroke, and butterfly, positions him as a formidable force in the swimming arena.

Personal Life and Family

In 2021, Dressel married his wife Meghan, marking a significant milestone in his personal life. Future Prospects
