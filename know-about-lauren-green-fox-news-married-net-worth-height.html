Lauren Green is a familiar face at the Fox News, who has been serving on the network for more than two decades. She is a Chief Religion Correspondent for the Fox News Channel based in the New York bureau.

Lauren Green Bio; Family, Education, Height

The Minneapolis native was born as Lauren Susan Green on 30 June 1958 as a daughter of parents Robert F. Green and Bessie Grissam Green. Her family consists of two sisters, Barbara and Lois, and two brothers, Leslie and Kenneth.

Lauren with the dashing height of 5 feet 11 inches (1.80 meters) received Bachelor of Music in piano performance from the University of Minnesota. Later, she went to the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University.

Her Career, Fox News, Net Worth

Initially, Lauren was a general assignment reporter at ABC-affiliated KSTP-TV in St. Paul, Minnesota, joining in 1988. After working for five years, she moved to CBS-affiliated WBBM-TV and took the responsibility as a weekend news anchor and correspondent.

In 1996, she joined Fox News Channel and worked on various platforms of the network. She as a guest panelist on its late-night/early-morning satirical talk show, Red Eye w/ Greg Gutfeld. Similarly, she was a headline anchor on Fox & Friends, a daily morning conservative news/talk program.

In addition, she released a book, Lighthouse Faith: God As a Living Reality in a World Immersed in Fog in 2017.

Married To Attorney Husband In 2015

Lauren got married to attorney Ted Nikolis on 2nd May 2015. The Big Fat Greek Orthodox Cathedral wedding ceremony was held in New York City.

Ted serves as Of Counsel at Merle, Brown & Nakamura, P.C., a full-service law firm located in downtown New York. Moreover, the New York University School of Law graduate is the director of Merchant Ivory International.

The couple first met at Cathedral Greek Church and started dating. Later, the 60-year-old got engaged with Ted in the same year of their wedding in 2015.
