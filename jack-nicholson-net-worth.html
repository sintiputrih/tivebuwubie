Early Life

Jack Nicholson, originally John Joseph Nicholson, was born on April the 22nd, 1937, in Neptune City, New Jersey, Unites States.

His mother, June Frances Nicholson, was a showgirl of Irish, German, English, and Welsh descent.

His mother was married to an Italian-American showman without realizing he was already married.

The true identity of his biological father is unknown, but some speculations have been made that he might have been the son of his mother’s manager during her time as a showgirl.

As she was only seventeen years of age and unmarried, his parents agreed to raise Jack as their own child, not revealing his true background, and his mother would act as his sister.

He was unknown about his relations with his mother and aunt, but in 1974 Time magazine researchers told him that his sister June’s true relation was his mother and his other sister was really his aunt.

But by this time, his mother and grandmother had died.

Growing up in Neptune City, Jack was raised in his mother’s Roman Catholic Church. Jack went to close Manasquan High School. He was declared “Class Clown” by the class of 1954.
