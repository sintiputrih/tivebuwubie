Netflix’s The Girlfriend Who Didn’t Exist: Meet Ronaiah Naya Tuiasosopo, Manti Te’o Catfisher

Yet, she does wishes that the whole thing with Te’o and Lennay never happened. All this she confessed in the Netflix documentary. Still, the character Lennay did her some good. She explained how it helped her figure out some stuff about herself.

In between, Naya also contacted Te’o to confess and apologize. While Te’o, he ends the Netflix documentary by saying he forgives her.

What Is Ronaiah Naya Tuiasosopo Doing Today?

Anyway, Naya seemed to have moved on with her life since. She even found clarity over her sexuality and found passion in Siva Samoa and the Polynesian culture.

Is Ronaiah Naya Tuiasosopo A Transgender?

During one of these occasions, she shared that it took her a lot of dark and painful seasons to be able to stand boldly today, with the wind at her back and the warmth of the sun on her skin that she is proud to be in.
