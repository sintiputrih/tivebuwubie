Timeline

Osborn was selected in the fifth round of the 2020 NFL Draft with the 176th pick by the Minnesota Vikings.

Osborn is a native of Ypsilanti, Michigan and attended Lincoln High School before transferring to IMG Academy for his senior year. Osborn played at Buffalo for three years. He had 96 catches for 1,490 yards and 12 touchdowns. He transferred to Miami as a graduate transfer in 2019. He led the Hurricanes with 50 receptions for 547 yards and five touchdowns in addition to returning 16 punts for 255 yards (15.9 average) and 10 kickoffs for 201 yards (20.1 average). Osborn earned second team All-MAC honors.

Kendrick "K. J." Osborn Jr. (born June 10, 1997) is an American football wide receiver for the Minnesota Vikings of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Miami.
