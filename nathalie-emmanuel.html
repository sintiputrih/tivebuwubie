Nathalie Emmanuel (born March 2, 1989) is a Britsh Actress, Model and Instagram Star from Southend-on-Sea, Essex, England. She initially gained success with her recurring role of Sasha Valentine in the TV series “Hollyoaks” in 2006.

In fact, this was her debut show and earlier she acted in various West End theatre shows. Furthermore, her major theatre work was in the musical “The Lion King” back in the late 1990s.

Nathalie appeared in her first show from 2006 to 2010. Meanwhile, the actress acted in shows like Hollyoaks Later and Hollyoaks: The Morning After the Night Before. Later, in 2011, she played the minor role of Cheryl Hallows in the series “Casualty”.

She performed in the episode “Only Human”. The same year, Emmanuel appeared in episode 3.1 as Charlie in the show “Misfits”. n addition, the actress made her silver screen debut with the movie “Twenty8k” as in 2012.

Then, in 2013, she landed with one of the major roles of Missandei in the super hit show “Game of Thrones“. Recently, it was announced that her character will die in season 8 of the show.

Nathalie’s character first introduced as a slave in season 3 who was later rescued by Daenerys. During an interview, she praised her death scene and said it was a fantastic scene to play.

In 2015, she played one of the important roles of Megan Ramsey in the hit franchise “Furious 7” movie. The actress reprised the role in 2017 in the film “The Fate of the Furious”. In 2015, she also performed in “Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials” as Harriet.

Profile, Parents & Before Fame

This Actress was born on the 2nd of March 1989 in Southend-on-Sea, Essex, England, United Kingdom. Hence, Nathalie Emmanuel’s age is 30 years old, as of 2019. Her father is of half-Saint Lucian and half English descent.

Nathalie’s mother is of a half Dominican (Dominiquais) and half English. Therefore, the actress belongs to multiracial ethnicity. She also has an elder sister named Louise Emmanuel.

At an early age, she started her acting training at the independent St Hilda’s School. In fact, she played Young Nala in the West End production of the musical “The Lion King” At the age of 10. Later, Emmanuel graduated from Westcliff High School for Girls.
