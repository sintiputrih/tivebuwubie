Cameron Alexander Dallas: Wiki Biography

Born on the 8th September 1994 in Whittier, California USA, Cameron Alexander Dallas is of part-Scottish, German, and Mexican descent. He is a social media personality and vlogger, best recognized for posting his videos on his official Vine channel. He is also known as a singer and actor and has been active in the entertainment industry since 2012.

Have you ever wondered how rich Cameron Dallas is? According to authoritative sources, his net worth is estimated to be over $1.2 million as of late 2017, accumulated through his successful involvement in the entertainment industry. Another source of income comes from the sales of his clothing line.

Cameron Dallas Net Worth $1.2 million

Cameron Dallas spent his childhood in Chino, California, where he was raised with an older sister by his mother, Gina Dallas.

Cameron’s career began in 2012 when he created an account on Vine, a popular application for short videos. He gained popularity by posting videos frequently, depicting him playing pranks on his family and friends. This marked the beginning of his net worth. During the same period, he also created his official Twitter account, on which he has gained more than 11 million followers. Additionally, Cameron also launched his official Instagram profile, on which he has garnered more than 21 million followers. Moreover, he has also launched a self-titled YouTube channel, on which he has almost six million subscribers and posts videos about his life.

Apart from his career as a social media personality, Cameron has also become known as an actor. He made his film debut in the role of Felix O’Neil in the 2014 film “Expelled”. In the following year, he guest-starred in two episodes of the NBC thriller series “American Odyssey”, after which he won the role of Frankie Payton in the film “The Outfield” (2015). All of these roles added a considerable amount to his net worth.

Cameron appears in his own reality TV show “Chasing Cameron”, which premiered on Netflix in December of 2016, further contributing to his net worth.

Besides that, Cameron has also tried himself as a singer, releasing his debut single entitled “She Bad” in April 2015. The song peaked at No. 46 on the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart, increasing further his net worth.

Thanks to his accomplishments in the entertainment industry, Cameron has won several awards, including the Teen Choice Award in the category for Most Popular Viner in 2014 and 2015, the 2016 Teen Choice Award for Choice Social Media King, and the 43rd People’s Choice Award in the category of Favorite Social Media Star in 2017.

Regarding his personal life, Cameron Dallas dated model Chase Carter in 2016, and briefly had a relationship with Hailey Baldwin. He is currently single.
