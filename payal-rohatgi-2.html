Some Lesser Known Facts About Payal Rohatgi

  • Payal is an ardent devotee of Shirdi Sai Baba.
  • She is a fitness freak and won numerous medals in the sports competition in her school & college.
  • In 2000, she participated in the Femina Miss India beauty pageant with Priyanka Chopra, Lara Dutta, and Dia Mirza.
  • She won the title of Supermodel Miss Tourism World 2001 and Miss India Tourism 2001.
  • After that, she started modeling for popular brands like Amul, Nescafe, Nirma, Jaguar Bath Panel, Dabur Hair Oil, etc.
  • She featured in some commercial advertisements like Cadbury’s Temptations Chocolates, Dabur Lal Dant Manjan with cricketer Virender Sehwag,  Eveready battery with Amitabh Bachchan, etc.
  • She also appeared in some music videos by Silk Route, Rock Band, and Indipop artist KK (Krishnakumar Kunnath).
  • She got a breakthrough role in 2002 in the Bollywood film ‘Yeh Kya Ho Raha Hai?’ as Esha.
  • She worked in different languages like Hindi, English, and Telugu.
  • She participated in many reality shows like ‘Bigg Boss ‘Season 2 (2008), ‘Raaz Pichhle Janam Ka’ (2009), ‘Zor Ka Jhatka: Total Wipeout’ (2011), ‘Survivor India’ (2012) and ‘Nach Baliye’ Season 7 (2015) along with her fiance Sangram Singh.
  • She was the winner of the reality show ‘Welcome – Baazi Mehmaan Nawazi Ki’ (2013).
  • In 2013, she appeared in the Salman Khan‘s famous reality show ‘Bigg Boss’ Season 7 to support Sangram Singh (Wrestler).
  • She is the brand ambassador of International Human Rights Organisation (IHRO).
