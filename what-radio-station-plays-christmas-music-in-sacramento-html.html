What station is mix 96?

KYMX (96.1 FM, “Mix 96”) is a commercial radio station that is licenced to Sacramento, California, United States. The station is owned by Bonneville International and broadcasts an adult contemporary format. KYMX’s transmitter is located in Natomas and its studios are in North Sacramento.
