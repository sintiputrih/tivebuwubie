Ralo - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-03-22

Atlanta rapper who founded his own label called Famerica. He gained recognition from his single "Can't Lie," featuring rapper Future in 2015 and went on to release mixtapes such as Famerican Gangster and Diary of the Streets. 

Before Fame

Growing up, he planned to be an electrician. He got started in music after being incarcerated, and out of spite for the woman he was seeing at the time who was treating him poorly.

Trivia

He has worked with rappers such as Shy Glizzy, Rich Homie Quan, Bandit Gang Marco, Young Thug and more.

Family Life

He was born Terrell Davis in Atlanta, Georgia. He had to take care of his family as the man of the house growing up.

Associated With

He has also been signed to rapper Young Scooter's Black Migo Gang.
