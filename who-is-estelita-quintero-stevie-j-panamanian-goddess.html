Publish date: 2024-03-15

1. She’s a model/actress

Estelita Quintero labels herself as a model/actress on Instagram. However, Stevie J is a music manager, so it’s unclear if he’s helping her kickstart a career in that industry, or simply taking on a wider range of clients these days. When he first introduced her on his Instagram, he referred to her as a “salsa artist.”

2. She’s already been on an episode of ‘Love & Hip-Hop: Atlanta’

Estelita was first introduced on the May 8 episode of Love & Hip-Hop: Atlanta as Stevie’s “Panamanian Goddess”. While Stevie and Joseline Hernandez seem to have reconciled their relationship recently, at the this this footage were filmed, they were definitely broken up. However, Estelita insisted on the episode that her relationship with Stevie was strictly for business.

3. She has acting credits

On her IMDB page, it’s revealed that Estelita starred in a short called The Get Away and TV movie called Trust. She also has an project called 3am In Hollywood that’s in post-production, so we’ll see more of her acting soon.
