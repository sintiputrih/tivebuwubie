Beginnings in the life of Nicolás Haza

Nicolás Haza was born on November 11, 1999 in Mexico. His parents are the renowned actress Ludwicka Paleta and Plutarco de la Haza. As for his family life we must point out that he has been considered as a very affectionate older brother, since he maintains a very good relationship with the children that his parents have had with their respective partners, since they are currently separated.

On his mother’s side, Ludwika Paleta, Nicolás Haza has Barbara and Sebastian as siblings, whom his mother had as a result of her relationship with Emiliano Salinas. While on his father’s side, Plutarco Haza has as a half-brother little Leonardo, who was born in 2019 as a result of Plutarco Haza’s relationship with Ximena del Toro. At this point it is important to mention that Nicolás Haza is especially close to his mother, next to whom he shares a lot of content on his Instagram social network account.

From an early age, partly due to the influence of his mother, Nicolás Haza was interested in the world of entertainment, so he moved to Canada, where he studied a career in cinematography in Toronto.

However, shortly after he realized that his true passion was music, so it is considered that the beginnings of his artistic career occurred in 2021, when Nicolás Haza launched his first known single under the name of “Trenze”, which was followed by the musical themes of: “No te dolió”, “Flying high”, “Navagando”, “Odisea” and “Vuelta al mundo”; although at first Nicolás Haza became known within music as Nico Haza but then finally decided to call himself “Niico”.

Regarding the beginnings of his artistic career, in different interviews Nicolás Haza has commented on how he knew he wanted to dedicate himself to music within the genre of trap and hip hop: “Since I was little I started playing the guitar, I have always been involved in music, as much as cinema, I studied cinema but music never left my life, It was always there, so it’s my way of integrating music and doing what I like.”
