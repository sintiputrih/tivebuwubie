Kane County Flea Market | Enjoy Illinois

Publish date: 2024-03-16

There is nothing like holding that new found item in your hands. No matter if you have been coming to the Flea Market for over 40 years or just discovered us, we know you'll have a great time.

Do you live for collecting? Are you are searching for that perfect piece of jewelry, furniture, books, antiques, or fancy "junque?" Perhaps you just need a day outing with the family that is cheaper than a movie? Well, the Kane County Flea Market is the place for you! There is nothing like holding that new found item in your hands. No matter if you have been coming to the Flea Market for over 40 years or just discovered us, we know you'll have a great time. We believe somewhere among our 100's of indoor, outdoor, and under shed vendors, we have what you're looking for!

On our website, you will find information for first-timers who have been bitten by the "collecting bug", as well as information for seasoned veterans! No matter if you just want to come and shop or become a dealer, this is the place to start your adventure. We were just named as one of the 10 Best Flea Markets in the World by Jet Setter Magazine. We are ranked with markets in Oslo, Paris and London. All the buyers and sellers always knew this to be true, and now the rest of the world does too.
