WWE Main Event Spoilers (Taped On 10/30)

Publish date: 2024-07-28

WWE taped matches for the November 2 episode of WWE Main Event from the Bon Secours Wellness Arena ahead of WWE RAW on October 30.

The spoilers, courtesy of WrestleZone’s Colby Applegate, are as follows:

  • Katana Chance def. Blair Davenport
  • Akira Tozawa def. Wes Lee

WrestleZone will have coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on October 30.
