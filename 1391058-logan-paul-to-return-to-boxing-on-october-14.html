Logan Paul To Return To Boxing On October 14

Publish date: 2024-07-10

WWE star Logan Paul will make his return to boxing later in 2023.

Per Marc Raimondi of ESPN, Paul is set to fight on October 14. While his opponent has bene decided, the bout has not officially been announced. The DAZN pay-per-view event will be named “The Prime Card” and it will emanate from Manchester, England. Raimondi noted that KSI, Paul’s business partner, will also fight on the card.

Speaking with ESPN, Paul emphasized his excitement.

“I’m f—ing pumped, man,” Paul said. “It’s been so long since I had a boxing fight and I feel like I’ve kind of been left out of the conversation. But I don’t train wrestling, I train boxing.”

Paul has not boxed since June 2021. He battled Floyd Mayweather Jr. to a draw in an exhibition bout. Paul lost his only professional fight against KSI.

In recent months, Paul has been regularly involved with WWE. He will face Ricochet at WWE SummerSlam on August 5.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.

Logan Paul Is Set For WWE RAW

WWE announced that Logan Paul will appear on the July 31 episode of WWE RAW. Both Paul and Brock Lesnar have been announced for the show. Monday’s episode will be the go-home show ahead of WWE SummerSlam.

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on Monday.
