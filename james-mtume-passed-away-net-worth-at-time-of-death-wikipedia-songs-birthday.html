James Mtume was an American musician, songwriter, radio personality, and record producer who was active in the R&B and jazz genres. James Mtume and his band members bagged the Grammy Award for Best R&B Song in 1981.

James Mtume was born and raised in South Philadelphia and he attended Pasadena City College in Pasadena, California.
