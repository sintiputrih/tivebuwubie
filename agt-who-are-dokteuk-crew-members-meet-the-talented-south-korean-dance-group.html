AGT: Who Are Dokteuk Crew Members? Meet The Talented South Korean Dance Group

Publish date: 2024-07-14

If you are also wondering who are the Dokteuk Crew in AGT, they are a skilled dance group from South Korea. 

Dokteuk Crew has made an impressive entrance in America’s Got Talent season 16.

The crew performed an anime-themed dance which was rather new on the American show. 

The South Korean dance group received a standing ovation from the judges after their very complicated yet simply executed dance was over. 

Dokteuk Crew is a professional dancing group in South Korea. They have also done much collaboration with brands such as Sony Music Korea. 

Let us learn more about Dokteuk Crew and take a closer look at the members and their ages. 

Who Are Dokteuk Crew On AGT?

Well, the bar for the dance performances on AGT season 16 has been raised high by the South Korean Dokteuk Crew. 

The crew was able to impress judges and receive a standing ovation after their anime-themed dance performance. 

They also managed to showcase some martial arts moves with smooth anime-inspired storytelling along with other dancing performances. 

Dokteuk Crew also has a YouTube channel, where we can find their original choreography and dance videos.

Dokteuk means unique and they have lived up to their name in the audition of AGT. 

Dokteuk Crew Members Names 

Dokteuk Crew features six male members and one female member. 

The names of the male Dokteuk Crew members are Jongwook Kim, Jang Yo-han, J – DOK, KYUKANG KIM, Hyemin Lee, Jeong Se-hong. 

And, the female member of the Dokteuk Crew is named Khaki. 

All the crew members hail from South Korea and they are currently representing their country in AGT season 16. 

Dokteuk Crew Members Age- How Old Are They?

Dokteuk Crew members have not revealed their actual ages as of now. 

However, they all seem to share a common age group judging by their appearance. 

They seem to fall into the age group of 25-30 years old. 

Meet The Dokteuk Crew On Instagram

The Dokteuk Crew is found on Instagram with their username @dokteuk_crew. 

They have amassed over 20.7k followers with 222 posts so far.
