Wheel of Fortune contestant insults Pat Sajak for 'filling space' after host mocks player's skill

Publish date: 2024-08-27

A WHEEL of Fortune contestant has insulted Pat Sajak for filling space after being mocked for their skill.

Pat welcomed a contestant named Joel Crandall to Wheel of Fortune in a clip on the show's official TikTok account.

Pat, 77, found out about the guest's prowess at the game of Simon Says.

“People like to brag about their skills,” the host said during the clip.

"I’m not sure I would brag about this when you’re an expert at doing Simon Says," he continued.

Joel had an answer ready for Pat's putdown.

VALEN-TIME OUT

Last week, Pat roasted a married couple that fumbled a puzzle.

The comical mishap came amid the weeklong Sweethearts Special, where couples team up to tackle the iconic game show.

Dameron and John faced off against Joe and Amy and Christy and Trent on Wednesday’s episode.

Despite being parents of three and married for a remarkable 33 years (with Joe having originally dated Christy's sister), the seasoned couple encountered a major stumble during the triple-toss-ups.

The puzzle, a two-word challenge under the category "Thing," seemed straightforward as the letters unveiled themselves automatically.

Confidently, Christy's husband whispered a response to her, prompting her to ring in with the guess: "Investment firm?"

Pat, 77, with a drawn-out tone, quashed their optimism with a simple "No."

In a twist of irony, Joe and Amy swooped in with the correct answer, "Engagement Ring," earning a playful jab from Pat.

With a wry smile, he teased, "You were romantic, they were just practical."

Meanwhile, high school sweethearts Joe and Amy emerged victorious, securing a spot in the bonus puzzle.

Their triumph continued as they conquered the puzzle as "Take a New Approach."

The couple walked away with a whopping $40,000 bonus prize, bringing their total Valentine's Day haul to an impressive $64,341.
