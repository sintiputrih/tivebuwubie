Larry Hornberger of Mifflinburg Dies After Battling Cancer

Publish date: 2024-05-19

The discomfort in my chest has persisted even after I spent the entire afternoon and evening thinking about it. I’m not sure why that is.

It was discovered that Larry Hornberger, a close relative of mine who had been given a cancer diagnosis, had passed away.

We discussed the events that occurred to us as children. Every every week of the summer and every single break from  school, we spent there with our grandparents.

Both the summer and the breaks were included in this. Just like his brother Bill is, having him around the house was like having another brother. It was like having a second brother to have him around the house. I’m sobbing for him as well as for myself.

I hope that now that you are gone, Larry, you can finally rest in peace.

Your Mommom, Poppop, and Aunt Lillie, who is also known as your Mama, are eagerly anticipating your arrival at the compound’s entrance. Sometimes people will refer to your Aunt Lillie as your Mother. Till we meet again as a group, please be sure to keep a close watch on all of our loved ones. Please remember him and his family in your prayers at this trying time. There will be a lot of people who mourn the absence of Larry from their life.
