The Pass Movie 2023 Todd Tucker Release Date and Cast

Publish date: 2024-08-14

The Pass 2023 movie cast includes Drew Sidora, Robert Christopher Riley, and Erica Peeples in the lead roles. Charmin Lee and Deetta West will also appear in the movie.

Drew Sidora

American actress and singer Sidora plays the lead role of the wife, who gets to embark on the sensual experience in The Pass.

Describing her role as "challenging," she shared fans will learn more about the film in this season of RHOA. "'It's definitely very juicy, very juicy," Drew Sidora added.

The actress is well-known for her recurring role as Chantel in the popular Disney Channel Original series That's So Raven.

Other notable roles include Lucy Avila in the 2006 film Step Up, Paige in the television series Hindsight, and Tionne Watkins in the VH1 TLC biographical film CrazySexyCool: The TLC Story.

She was added to the cast of Bravo's The Real Housewives of Atlanta in Season 13. Drew made her debut in December 2020.

Married to Ralph Pittman, the couple share two kids: a son, Machai (born in June 2015), and a daughter, Aniya (February 2018). She also has a son named Josiah from a previous relationship.

On February 27, 2023, the actress filed a petition to divorce her husband.

Robert Christopher Riley

Riley is an American film, television, and stage actor. He is notable for portraying Terrence Wall in the VH1 drama series Hit The Floor from 2013 to 2016.

On Broadway, the actor appeared as Dave Robinson in Lombardi and starred as Brick in the 2009 production of Tennessee Williams's Cat On A Hot Tin Roof.

In 2014, he played the role of Jay "The Sport" Jackson in the Old Globe Theatre's production of Marco Ramirez's The Royale. He also appeared in the 2016 TV One movie Bad Dad Rehab as Jared.

In March 2017, Robert was cast in The CW drama series Dynasty reboot as chauffeur Michael Culhane.

His other notable acting credits include The Perfect Match (2016), Hit the Floor (2013-2016), Underground (2017), Elementary (2017), The Game (2022-2023), and True Lies (2023).

Erica Peeples

Peeples is an actress, poet, producer, and DJ. She earned her BFA at the world-renowned performing arts conservatory, Julliard.

After completing the program, she starred in several off-Broadway regional productions, such as August Wilson's Joe Turner's Come and Gone and Lorraine Hansberry's A Raisin in the Sun.

She made her television debut in 2007, playing the role of Samantha Joosten in Law & Order: Criminal Intent.

Peeples then moved to Los Angeles, California, where she landed her breakout role, starring in the hit feature film "True to the Game" alongside Vivica A. Fox, Nelsan Ellis, and Columbus Short.

Following her major theatrical debut, the actress has since gone on to appear in a variety of productions, including Fall Girls (2019) and Gully (2019).

She appeared in a recurring role as Mo on CW's All American Seasons 2 and 3.

Other notable appearances include True to the Game (2017), Trigger (2020), Flint Tale (2021), Haus of Vicious (2022), and On Ten (2022-2023).
