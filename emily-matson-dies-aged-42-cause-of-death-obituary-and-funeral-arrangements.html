Emily Matson dies aged 42: cause of death, obituary and funeral arrangements

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Emily Matson was a respected reporter and anchor for local news in Erie, Pennsylvania. Her career spanned almost twenty years; she started as an intern at WJET-TV, then moved to WICU 12 to create the morning show before going on to become an on-air reporter.

Emily Matson’s cause of death

Matson was well-known in Erie’s broadcast journalism and was renowned for her love and enthusiasm for the city. Her last on-air appearance was on Friday, and her death was reported by Erie News Now, the news station where she had worked for nearly two decades. Her death’s cause has not been made public.

Matson’s friends and coworkers highlighted her lively personality and the good influence she had on the newsroom as they voiced their shock at her unexpected passing. Her kindness, inclusion, and assistance for those just starting in the field were remembered by her coworkers. In addition, admirers and fans paid tribute to her, recalling her contributions to the 7 o’clock news and expressing the profound vacuum that her loss has left.

Emily Matson’s obituary and funeral arrangements

A memorial event will be held in Emily Matson’s honor to celebrate her life and legacy.
Details about her funeral arrangements and obituary will be available as soon as it’s ready.
