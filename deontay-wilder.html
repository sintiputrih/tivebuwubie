Deontay Wilder Height, Age, Wife, Net worth, Biography, Family &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Deontay Wilder (born October 22, 1985) is an American Professional Boxer from Tuscaloosa, Alabama. He has held the “World Boxing Council” (WBC) heavyweight title since 2015. Furthermore, the 33-year-old boxer is popular for being one of the longest-reigning world heavyweight champions.

Wilder started his amateur boxing career in October 2005. Moreover, he joined “Skyy Boxing Gym” in Northport, Alabama. Deontay trained under Jay Deas. In 2007, he defeated the world champion named “Isiah Thomas”.

Later, Wilder registered his career-best score by defeating Rakhim Chakhiyev in 2008. During the Summer Olympics, the boxer was able to win the bronze medal. Deontay made his debut in professional boxing on November 15, 2008. Wilder fought at the Vanderbilt University Memorial Gymnasium in Tennessee.

Moreover, in 2012, the boxer won his first professional boxing title. He defeated Kelvin Price at the “Memorial Sports Arena” in Los Angeles. On January 17, 2015, he fought the defending champion, Bermane Stiverne. The fight was held at the “MGM Grand Garden Arena” in Las Vegas. Wilder won the ‘WBC’ heavyweight title after defeating Stiverne.

Recently, Deontay challenged Tyson Fury (British Professional Boxer) at Los Angeles’ Staples Center. Both the champions held an impressive record. The match will be held on this Saturday i.e. December 1, 2018.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The professional boxer was born on 22nd October 1985 in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, United States. Therefore, Deontay Wilder’s age is 33 years old, as of 2018. The boxer’s father’s name is Gary Wilder and his mother’s name is Deborah Wilder.

Furthermore, he attended Tuscaloosa Central High School and passed the school in 2004. Later, Deontay graduated from the Shelton State Community College. The boxer belongs to Afro-American ethnicity.

During his school, Wilder wanted to become a basketball player because of his impressive height 6 Feet 6 Inches (201 centimeters).

Personal Affairs, Girlfriend & Children

During high school, Wilder started his relationship with girlfriend “Helen Duncan”. When he was just 19-year-old, his partner gave birth to their daughter “Naieya Wilder”. However, his baby girl was born with spina bifida.

Later, she has undergone various surgeries and now Naieya is absolutely normal. Furthermore, the couple later broke up due to some personal reasons. The boxer then married his wife named “Jessica Scales-Wilder” in 2009.

Jessica gave birth to their three children. Firstly, they welcomed their daughter Ava and then two sons, Deontay Jr., and Dereon Wilder. Moreover, their marriage later ended in divorce.

Currently, Deontay is engaged to his fiancee “Telli Swift” (American model and TV personality). They started their affair in 2017. The couple welcomed their first child “Kaorii Lee Wilder” on February 06, 2018.
