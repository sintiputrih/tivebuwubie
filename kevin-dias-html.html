Kevin Dias - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-01-28

French TV actor known for playing Benoit in season two of the Netflix show Emily in Paris. He is popular on Instagram and has over 40,000 followers. 

Before Fame

He got his start in the popular 2001 movie Amélie. 

Trivia

He is a musician and artist. He plays guitar and does artwork with neon lights with the group The Milaniz.

Family Life

He is from France. He is dating model Margot Milani. He has a son named Milo. 

Associated With

He acted as Ashley Park's romantic partner on Emily in Paris.
