Who is Zach Bryans Wife? Get to Know the Dating History

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Zach Bryan, the well-known American musician and former naval officer, has captured the hearts of many with his hit songs like “Oklahoma Smokeshow” and “Condemned.” Apart from his successful career, fans have been curious about his personal life, especially his current wife, after parting ways with Rose Madden. So, who is Zach Bryan’s wife now?

Zach Bryan’s Background

Zach Bryan, born on April 2, 1996, in Okinawa, Japan, comes from a military family. His father, Dewayne Bryan, served as a master chief in the military. However, his parents separated when he was around 12 years old. Sadly, his mother, Annette DeAnn, struggled with alcohol addiction and passed away on August 4, 2016. Zach and his sister, MacKenzie Taylor, grew up in Oklahoma, United States.

At 17, Zach Bryan enlisted in the United States Navy. Nevertheless, in October 2021, he decided to leave the Navy and pursue a music career, which has proven to be a remarkable choice. He gained widespread recognition with songs like “Something in the Orange” and “The Good I’ll Do.”

Rose Madden

Zach Bryan’s romantic journey led him to Rose Madden. They tied the knot in July 2020, but unfortunately, their marriage was short-lived, and by July 2021, they had divorced. This separation was evident when Rose wiped all pictures of Zach from her social media.

Deb Peifer

Following his split from Rose Madden, Zach moved on to a relationship with Deb Peifer. The two often shared glimpses of their life, including their adorable dog, with fans. However, in May 2023, Zach announced their breakup with a heartfelt message, emphasizing mutual respect and privacy during this challenging time.

Brianna LaPaglia

Zach Bryan’s love life took an exciting turn when he was linked to Barstool Sports personality Brianna LaPaglia, also known as Brianna Chickenfry. Their relationship sparked during one of Zach’s New York City concerts in June 2023. Brianna confirmed their relationship on her podcast, “PlanBri Uncut,” stating that it was fun and casual.

The couple spent more time together as the summer progressed, even in Zach’s hometown of Oklahoma. However, in September 2023, Zach faced legal troubles with an arrest for “obstruction of investigation,” Brianna playfully referenced this situation by sharing Britney Spears’ “Criminal” on her Instagram Story.

Are Zach Bryan and Rose Madden Still Married?

No, Zach Bryan and Rose Madden are no longer married. They divorced in July 2021. Rumors suggest that their marriage ended due to allegations of infidelity while Rose was deployed to Italy. Zach’s love life has since evolved, with Deb Peifer and, most recently, Brianna LaPaglia being part of his romantic journey.

Zach Bryan’s music career continues to flourish, and his personal life remains a topic of interest for his dedicated fanbase.
