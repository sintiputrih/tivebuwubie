Leslie Mann is an American actress who has established a prolific career, mainly in the comedy genre. She was first noticed for her role in The Cable Guy. Throughout the years, she has appeared in supporting and starring roles in several well-known feature films. Some career highlights are in Knocked Up, Drillbit Taylor, 17 Again, and Funny People. Her being incorporated to comedies and romantic comedies has something to do with her being married to Judd Apatow, who’s known for directing and producing such genres. She continued her prolific career in the 2010s with notable performances in The Change-Up, This Is 40, The Other Woman, and in the upcoming movie How to Be Single.
