Black Keys Drummer Patrick Carney Marries Emily Ward: See Their Wedding Pic!

Publish date: 2024-05-13

Patrick Carney and Emily Ward's Nashville nuptials were extra special, thanks to the creative couple's personal, down-home touches.

On Saturday, the Black Keys drummer, 32, and his girlfriend tied the knot at their home in Nashville in front of 350 guests, a rep for the rocker confirms to Us Weekly.

Saturday Night Live star Will Forte was on hand to officiate the backyard ceremony. For her trip down the aisle, Ward donned a lacy, cap-sleeve Carolina Herrera gown, and walked towards her groom to Tommy James & The Shondells' "Crimson and Clover." (The pair's Irish wolfhound, Charlotte, accompanied Carney during his walk to the altar.)

Carney's bandmate Dan Auerbach was in attendance, and the musician's brothers, Will and Michael Carney and Barry Stormer, along with Ward's siblings, comprised the wedding party.
