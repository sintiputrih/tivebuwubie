AEW Revolution Poster Pays Tribute to Stings Varied Gimmicks Throughout Career

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Sting shocked the world when he debuted in AEW in December 2020. Since then, he has been featured heavily on AEW television, being paired up with Darby Allin. Sting has been pulling off death-defying stunts since as early as AEW Revolution 2021 and he will be retiring soon. Now the official poster for AEW Revolution featured all of Sting’s gimmicks over the years.

Despite being 64 now, Sting has been competing at an unexpectedly high level, which has impressed fans and pro wrestlers alike. Over the years, the Stinger has performed various insane spots, including jumping from the ladder onto a table, among other things.

Sting will be ending his in-ring career at AEW Revolution next year, as fans wonder who his final opponent will be. AEW dropped the official poster for AEW Revolution, which comprised all of Sting’s previous gimmicks – surfer, crow, joker and more.

Tony Khan also aims for Sting to appear at AEW All In next year as an ambassador after retirement. It remains to be seen what will become of Sting in the end, as fans would love for him to have a proper retirement match – something that many feel WWE deprived him of, all those years ago.
