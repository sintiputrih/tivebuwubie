Whatever Happened to the Cast of The Days and Nights of Molly Dodd, Including Star Blair Brown

Publish date: 2024-02-21

Created by Jay Tarses, The Days and Nights of Molly Dodd was a dramedy series that undoubtedly served as a vehicle for its star, Blair Brown. In fact, the actress earned five Emmy nominations for playing the titular role of the equally yuppie and bohemian divorcée — one for each season it aired.

The first season of the show aired on NBC from 1987-1988 before it moved over to Lifetime and aired its other four seasons from 1988-1991. Aside from Blair, the cast included folks like William Converse-Roberts, Richard Lawson, David Strathairn and Maureen Anderman as the people in Molly’s life.

Since it has been about 30 years since The Days and Nights of Molly Dodd lit up the small screen, we figured it was about time we caught up with everyone is up to these days.
