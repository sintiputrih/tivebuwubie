Corey Carrier’s Net Worth

Corey Carrier is an American actor with a net worth of $100 thousand. He is best known for his role as Henry “Indiana” Jones, Jr. in the TV series “The Young Indiana Jones Chronicles”.

Carrier was born on August 20, 1980, in Middleborough, Massachusetts. He made his on-screen debut in 1987’s “When the Time Comes” as Jess. He appeared in several films including “The Witches of Eastwick” and “Nixon”.

Carrier portrayed the young Indiana Jones, a character made famous by Harrison Ford, when the character was between eight and ten years old. He reprised the role for “The Adventures of Young Indiana Jones: Travels with Father” four years later in 1996.

Carrier was active in baseball, fencing, guitar, basketball, and gymnastics as a child. He studied at Worcester’s Clark University before quitting the entertainment industry in 2000 after his final appearance in “When the Time Comes”.
