Who is Jacob Tremblay?

Jacob Tremblay is a Canadian actor and model born in British Columbia, Canada. He is well knowing for his appearance in movies like Good Boys, Wonder, The Shining and many others.

He finished his early education at Gordon Greenwood Elementary School, after that, he was homeschooled to concentrate on his acting career along with his studies.

He made his first tv appearance with the show called “Motive” in 2013. He also appeared in several tv shows like Santa’s Little Ferrets, American Dad, Animals, Mr Young and many more.

After that, he played his first movie role in a comedy animated movie titled The Smurfs 2, in which he played the role of Blue Winslow in the year 2013. In the same year, he also played a major role in the short movie titled ‘The Magic Ferret’, by portraying the role of Sam.
