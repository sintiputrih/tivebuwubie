Thomas “Uncle Tommy” Miles, born on May 18, 1967, received a bachelor’s degree in theatre from Texas A&M University. In addition to pursuing his degree, he joined the Royal Shakespeare Company. Thomas was cast in multiple parts by the theater company.

Miles is also well-known for hosting radio programs. He joined Steve Harvey, Shirley Strawberry, and Carla Ferrell on The Steve Harvey Morning Show, a radio program that premiered in 2000 and continues to air.
