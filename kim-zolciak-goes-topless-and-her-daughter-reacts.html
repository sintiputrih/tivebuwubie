Kim Zolciak Goes Topless and Her Daughter Reacts

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Kim Zolciak doesn’t have time for tan lines.

When the Real Housewives of Atlanta star took to Instagram on Monday, February 12, to share photos from her romantic vacation with her husband, Kroy Biermann, one piece of clothing was noticeably absent. 

“Where is ur top?” Zolciak’s second eldest child, 16-year-old Ariana, wrote in the comments. Replied Zolciak, 39: “@arianabiermann it’s on you can’t see it?”

In the picture’s caption, Zolciak praised the the 32-year-old former NFL player, whom, she married in 2011. “Day 1 of our getaway,” she wrote. I would go to the end of this earth for you my love, my best friend, my strength, my heart and my world.”

Just two days before shedding her bikini top, Zolciak ditched her trademark wig and makeup.

“I feel beautiful with no makeup and no wig and I feel beautiful with makeup and a wig,” she shared with her more than 2 million Instagram followers. “I love being a woman, dressing up, and I also love running around in my La Perla pajamas bottoms (that are old as s—t I wear them everyday) and my bathrobe.”
