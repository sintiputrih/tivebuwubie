Kelly Osbourne Shaves Head, Keeps Mini-Mohawk in Makeup-Free Picture

Publish date: 2024-04-29

Her bob just wasn’t cutting it! Kelly Osbourne showed off an even more drastic hairstyle via Instagram on Friday, May 2. The E! Fashion Police cohost shaved the majority of her head, leaving only a mini-mohawk behind.

“Make up free and loving it!” the 29-year-old captioned. In the snapshot, Osbourne looks unrecognizable with her lavender locks swept back in an unkept mohawk. Later that night, the star joked about her new look while attending an event with brother Jack Osbourne. “#Twins?” she wrote, while showing off the pair’s similar haircuts. 

Osbourne has been changing up her appearance since last month. She first revealed a half-shaved bob on April 6. “Here is one pic of my new hair cut!!! What do you guys think?” the Sharknado 2 star wrote at the time. “I’m also sporting the #SpringTrend and have no bottom mascara on! It looks weird.”
