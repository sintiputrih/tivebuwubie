Television personality known for being on Breakfast Television Toronto on CityTV Toronto. After suffering an on-air panic attack in 2006, he has been an open advocate for awareness.

He got his degree at Canadore College in radio and television broadcasting and began his career at CKNY in North Bay. He then moved on to Barrie, Ontario, where he worked at The New VR for nine years. He moved to Toronto and CityTV in 1991.

He ends his broadcasts with a wink, which is meant as a "hello" to his wife. He began this tradition at The New VR.

He married Beth Frankish in 1988 and they have two children.

He hosts Breakfast Television Toronto with Dina Pugliese.
