Witney Carson, Dancing With the Stars Pro, Marries Carson McAllister

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Tying the knot on New Year’s Day! Dancing With the Stars pro Witney Carson married her high school sweetheart Carson McAllister on Friday, January 1, in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Carson and McAllister, both 22, said “I do” in front of more than 100 guests at the religious ceremony and large dinner and reception at Noah’s Event Venue in South Jordan, Utah.

"There will definitely be elements of the winter wonderland shoot in our wedding,” Carson told The Knot last month, referring to the wintry engagement shoot they did in mid-December. “We want it to be rustic, woodsy and romantic and I think when it all comes together it’ll be magical!”

In November, Carson opened up to Us Weekly about her wedding planning.

"I thought it would be more stressful with everything going on, but it's actually been really fun," she told Us. "And we finally have a venue and that was the most stressful part. So it's going to be in Utah and it's just going to be really beautiful."

Guests at their special day included fellow DWTS pro and bridesmaids Emma Slater, Lindsay Arnold, Brittany Cherry and Jenna Johnson, as well as her former dance partners Carlos Penavega, Chris Soules and Alfonso Ribeiro.

"They're like my sisters!" she told Us of her bridesmaids.

"Can't believe today is the day!!!" the dancer captioned an Instagram snap on Friday, January 1, in which she kissed her soon-to-be husband. "Finally get to be with my love forever and all eternity. Can't wait to share with all of you the details."

The bride — who documented her journey to the altar on her website — wore a dress designed by Rivini, complete with long lace sleeves. "It's really, really pretty. I love it," she told Us of her custom-designed gown.

"Yesterday was absolutely magical," Carson captioned an Instagram snap on Saturday, January 2, from the big day. "Seriously the best two days of my life! The celebration was so much fun, and I couldn't have asked for better! More photos coming."

Cherry shared an Instagram snap on the wedding day. "#McAllister Wedding 2016," she captioned the pic with her fellow bridesmaids.

Karina Smirnoff, Carson's fellow DWTS castmate, also shared a snap of the pair's wedding invitation on Saturday, January 2.

"Congratulations to this sweet and gorgeous couple on their big day," she wrote.

The couple got engaged last October, with Carson — who competed on the most recent season of the hit reality competition show with partner Bachelor contestant Soules — announcing the good news on Instagram.

"My love of four years @carson.mcallister proposed last night & I feel like the luckiest, happiest woman in the world," she wrote on October 3. "It was perfect, he's perfect #engaged."
