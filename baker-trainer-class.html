From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

A Baker (Japanese: ベーカリー Bakery) is a type of Trainer class introduced in Generation V. They are women with a chef's hat, tray, and apron. Bakers are sometimes seen living in pink trailers with all their cooking utensils and cooking appliances enclosed within. Some Bakers ask players for ingredients or assistance. They use a variety of different Pokémon, mainly ones that are related to food or food preparation. In the Battle Subway, some Bakers specialize in Grass- and Fire-type Pokémon benefiting from harsh sunlight.

Minigame

Village Bridge Restaurant
Village Sandwiches are our specialty!

Under the Village Bridge there is a small sandwich van called the Village Bridge Restaurant, which is noted for its specialty Berry sandwiches. This is the location of Baker Chris, whose battle details are shown above. In Black and White, once defeated, Baker Chris will ask the player to temporarily help out, triggering a minigame. The object is to remember the orders of the four customers, repeat them to Chris, and then serve them in the correct order. The player will receive a Lum Berry as a reward. This can be done once per day.

In Black 2 and White 2, the sandwich shop has been moved into one of the buildings; however, the minigame can no longer be played at it.

In the manga

Pokémon Adventures

Baker Chris makes her first appearance in Unraveling Mysteries, where she meets up with Black and White. She takes them to the recently created Battle Subway after overhearing that Black wanted to know why Marshal had come to Nimbasa City. When White decides to become a Trainer, Chris lends her some Poké Balls so that she can capture a wild Deerling.

Chris later appears at the Pokémon League alongside several other Trainers to help battle Team Plasma.
