Is RuPaul's Drag Race judge Carson Kressley married?

Publish date: 2024-04-28

CARSON Kressley has been apart of the RuPaul Drag Race franchise since 2015.

Carson, 51, has had a long career in show business, having appeared on programs like Queer Eye for the Straight Guy and How to Look Good Naked.

Is Carson Kressley married?

Carson Kressley has spoken on previous relationships, but it's thought that he is currently single.

In the past few years, there have been rumors that romantically linked him to Thom Filicia, whom he co-hosts Get a Room with Carson & Thom on Bravo.

However, the pair shut down the rumors and confirmed they were good friends.

"We kind of help [each other meet people],” Thom said on Bravo.

“It’s very funny, you know; it’s easier to talk to someone you’re not flirting with so I’m really good. I can help him out when he’s being a little shy.”

In 2016, Carson said he had previously been in a relationship with former Atlanta Falcons defensive lineman Esera Tavai "Mr Aloha" Tuaolo.

Esera came out two years after his retirement, but Carson didn't reveal how long the pair had dated for.

Speaking to Bravo TV after they split, Carson admitted that dating a footballer was “pretty sexy,” and that he considered marrying his former flame.

The pair reportedly have remained good friends.

Who are the other judges on RuPaul's Drag Race?

The RuPaul's Drag Rice judging panel consists of legendary drag star RuPaul, Michelle Visage, Carson Kressley and a weekly guest judge.

The season was filmed during the COVID-19 pandemic, relying on strict protocols, including isolation and testing of contestants, judges, and crew.

Guest judges on season 13 have included Scarlett Johansson, Anne Hathaway, Stuart Vevers, Char Margolis, Nicole Byer, Loni Love and Cynthia Erivo.
